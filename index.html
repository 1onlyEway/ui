About Detox Brand

Detox Brand is a clothing company that specializes in providing high-quality hoodies and sweatpants. Our mission is to offer comfortable and stylish clothing options for everyday wear.

Clothing Services

At Detox Brand, we offer a range of clothing services, including:

  • Hoodies
  • Sweatpants

Whether you're lounging at home or hitting the gym, our clothing is designed to meet your comfort and style needs.

Brand Guidelines

Detox Brand follows strict brand guidelines to ensure consistency and cohesion across all our products and communications. Some key elements of our brand guidelines include:

  • Use of bold, modern typography
  • Minimalistic design approach
  • Color palette consisting of earthy tones and vibrant accents
  • Logo placement and sizing specifications
  • Photography style emphasizing comfort and versatility

By adhering to these guidelines, we aim to create a recognizable and memorable brand identity that resonates with our audience.
